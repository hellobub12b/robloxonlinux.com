Hello! Follow these steps for getting roblox on linux!!!

Step no.1:Installing GrapeJuice

First, you are going to go to this site:

Click here to get started!

Once you have opened the link, scroll all the way down until you find the "documantation" link

Once you are in the "Documentation Home", in the "Installing GrapeJuice" section, below, there will be some links, click on the link that says "install from source".

Once you have clicked that button, you are going to copy the code from the site where it says Debian/Ubuntu and paste in your terminal!

In the terminal, once you have copied and pasted the code, you are going to press enter and type in your password

Once it is done installing,you are going to scroll down to the "Installing GrapeJuice" section.

Now that you are in this section, you will now copy the first block of code and paste it in your terminal, when it is finished installing, copy and paste the second block of code in your terminal!

Step no.2:Installing Wine

To install Wine, you will open your terminal and type "sudo apt install wine", once it says y/n, you are going to click y and then enter, wait until the dowload is complete.Now, you are going to click the on the nine dots on the bottom left corner and search up grapejuice, then open it.

Once you have it opened, you will click on the player button on the left, and click the create button on the top right corner.

Then, you are going to wait until the tiny roblox window finishes installing, next, all you have to do is go to roblox.com, login (if you have'nt already), and click on a game you want to play on roblox.

WARNING!

If roblox didnt launch, then you are going to open GrapeJuice,click on the player button, and enable the "Use Mesa OpenGL version override" switch

Then open roblox again and launch it and it should work!
